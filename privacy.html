1. Who we are

Grants Partner ("we", "us", "our") is an EU grant management and innovation consultancy currently in development, operating at grantspartner.com. We are the data controller responsible for the personal data described in this policy.

For any questions about how we handle your data, contact us at:
hello@grantspartner.com

2. What data we collect

When you register your interest through our website, we collect:

  • Full name — to address you personally
  • Organisation name — optional, to understand your context
  • Email address — to contact you when we launch
  • Date and time of registration — for our records

We do not collect financial data, special category data, or anything beyond the above. We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this website.

3. Why we collect it and our legal basis

We collect your data solely to contact you with information about our launch and services relevant to EU grant management.

The legal basis is your explicit consent under Article 6(1)(a) of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), given when you ticked the consent box on our registration form. We will not use your data for any other purpose without obtaining fresh consent.

4. How long we keep your data

We retain your data for up to 24 months from the date you register, or until you withdraw consent — whichever comes first. After this period, or upon your request, your data is permanently deleted.

5. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in a database hosted on Cloudflare infrastructure within the European Economic Area. Cloudflare acts as a data processor on our behalf under GDPR-compliant terms. We do not transfer your data to third-party services, advertisers, or any other recipients.

6. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to the processing of your data

To exercise any right, write to us at hello@grantspartner.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Right to lodge a complaint

If you believe we have not handled your data in accordance with applicable law, you may lodge a complaint with the data protection supervisory authority in your EU member state. A full list is available at edpb.europa.eu.

8. Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or any third-party tracking technologies. No data about your browsing behaviour is collected.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
